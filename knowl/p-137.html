Written solutions are more than just answers. A complete solution to a problem:
  1. clearly states what the answer to the problem is;
  2. explains why the claimed answer is indeed the correct answer;
  3. is grounded in sound mathematical reasoning that builds on and/or refers directly to results, Facts, or Activities that we have explored in class;
  4. is written for an audience of a typical classmate; that is, someone who is generally new to these ideas and has the resources and experiences provided in this Activity Book.
